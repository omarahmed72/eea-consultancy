Real Estate and Lease Disputes

The real estate sector is one of the most legally complex areas due to the high economic value of properties and associated disputes. Our firm brings extensive legal expertise in handling all property and lease matters, including verifying ownership documents, property registration, and protecting the rights of owners and tenants from disputes.


We represent clients in disputes involving co-ownership, usufruct rights, property partition, eviction, and claims related to tenants’ and landlords’ rights. We also provide preventive legal advice before engaging in real estate transactions, including drafting sale and lease agreements to ensure clarity of rights and obligations and reduce future disputes.
